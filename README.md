# Financial Models

A collection of financial models I've built over the years and will continue updating as new financial information becomes available.

The models were created in different contexts throughout my career. Some were built for my personal investment portfolio, others during my time at the Archway Investment Fund, and others while I was an intern at Fidelity Investments. They reflect my approach to financial modeling, valuation, and company analysis, and I hope to keep improving them over time.

You'll notice that a large portion of these models are in the healthcare sector, particularly biotechnology. That's because I've spent a significant amount of time researching and investing in biotech companies for my own portfolio, so many of my models naturally reflect that focus.

## Naming convention

Files follow the pattern `SJCA-<TICKER>-<YEAR>.xlsx`, for example `SJCA-NVDA-2025.xlsx` or `SJCA-LLY-2026.xlsx`:

- **SJCA** — a consistent prefix across all models.
- **TICKER** — the company's stock ticker. Non-US listings include an exchange or region suffix, such as `MRK-GR` (Xetra), `SAN-FP` (Euronext Paris), `ROG-VX` (SIX Swiss), `NOVOB` (Copenhagen), and `4502` (Tokyo).
- **YEAR** — the year the model is anchored to. Coverage currently spans 2015 through 2026, with most models from 2024 and 2025.
