# models

These are financial models I’ve built over the years and will continue updating as new financial information becomes available.

The models were created in different contexts throughout my career. Some were built for my personal investment portfolio, others during my time at Archway Investment Fund, and others while I was an intern at Fidelity Investments. They reflect my approach to financial modeling, valuation, and company analysis, and I hope to keep improving them over time.

You’ll notice that a large portion of these models are in the healthcare sector, particularly biotechnology. That’s because I’ve spent a significant amount of time researching and investing in biotech companies for my own portfolio, so many of my models naturally reflect that focus.

Feel free to use these models however you’d like. You’re welcome to modify them, build upon them, or use them as a starting point for your own research and investment analysis. If you find them useful or make improvements, contributions are always welcome.
